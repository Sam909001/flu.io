Frequently Asked Questions

What is FLUFFI token?
FLUFFI is a community-driven token built on the Solana blockchain. It aims to provide fast transactions and low fees while rewarding its community through airdrops and staking.
How do I qualify for the airdrop?
To qualify for the airdrop, you need to complete at least one social task (Twitter follow, Telegram join, or retweet) and have a compatible Solana wallet.
When will I receive my tokens?
Tokens will be distributed within 48 hours after the airdrop ends. You'll receive a notification in your wallet once the tokens have been sent.
Can I participate multiple times?
No, each wallet address can only participate once. However, you can increase your rewards by completing more tasks and referring friends.
